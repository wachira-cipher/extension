This is how the site works :-
. On the listing, there is a whatsapp button to open a chat with the agent on whatsapp followed by a telephone number button for the agent.
. There is a click to zoom button to open the full photo of the property on the listing;
. Next to the click to zoom button is a "click to view property details" which will lead to a page containing the full photo of the property and all details provided by property   owners.
> A interested party can also fill a form in the contact agent section
